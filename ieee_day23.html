About IEEE Day

IEEE Day is celebrated on October 3rd each year, marking the anniversary of the first time IEEE members gathered to share their technical ideas in 1884. It is a global event that unites IEEE members, engineers, scientists, and technology enthusiasts worldwide.

IEEE Day is a unique occasion that allows us to revel in technological innovation and pay tribute to IEEE's pivotal role in shaping the world we live in today. IEEE Day provides a platform to celebrate technological innovation and the contributions of IEEE to the advancement of society. It's a day to recognize the impact of electrical, electronics, and computer engineering on our daily lives.

IEEE Student Branch IIT Patna is a dynamic and thriving community within the Indian Institute of Technology Patna. Founded with the goal of promoting technological innovation and excellence, this student branch has become a hub for aspiring engineers and researchers.

IEEE Student Branch IIT Patna is proud to announce the celebration of IEEE Day, a momentous occasion that brings together our vibrant community of engineering enthusiasts. IEEE Day is a time to celebrate our passion for technology and innovation, and our student branch is dedicated to making it a memorable event. Stay tuned for a day filled with exciting activities, enlightening talks, and opportunities to connect with fellow tech enthusiasts. Join us as we celebrate IEEE Day and showcase the spirit of curiosity, creativity, and collaboration that drives us in the world of electrical, electronics, and computer engineering

Plenary Talks
IEEE Day brings together some of the brightest minds and experts in the field to inspire and educate. Our speaker sessions are a highlight of the event, offering participants a unique opportunity to gain insights from industry leaders and visionaries.
Research Symposium
The IEEE Day Symposium is a cornerstone of our celebration, offering a platform for in-depth exploration of the latest technological advancements and research breakthroughs. This intellectual gathering serves as a melting pot of ideas, fostering collaboration and innovation.
Re-Engineer
"Re-Engineer" is an exciting and hands-on activity that lies at the heart of our IEEE Day celebration. This event challenges participants to apply their engineering skills, creativity, and problem-solving abilities to tackle real-world problems and come up with innovative solutions.
